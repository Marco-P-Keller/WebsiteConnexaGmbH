Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09.01.2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the website of Connexa GmbH (hereinafter referred to as “we,” “us,” or “our”). We are committed to respecting your privacy and believe in maintaining transparent practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information on our website, including how we process, store, and protect any data that you provide or that may be automatically collected during your visit.

By using our website, you agree to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy. Please read it carefully to understand how we collect and use your information, and how you can manage your preferences.

2. No Collection of Personal Data

We do not actively collect or store personal data from our website visitors. Specifically:

  • No cookies or similar tracking technologies: We have disabled all cookie-tracking features, meaning that no cookies are placed on your device when you visit our website. This includes both first-party and third-party cookies.
  • No analytics or tracking scripts: We do not run any analytics services (such as Google Analytics) or tracking scripts that monitor user activity, page visits, or browsing behavior.
  • No personal information requests: We do not request or store any personal information (e.g., names, email addresses, or phone numbers) from our visitors for marketing or promotional purposes. Consequently, we do not maintain any form of user database for our website.

Our priority is to ensure that your privacy remains safeguarded and that you can browse our website anonymously without intrusive data collection or personalized advertising.

3. Hosting and Log Files

Although we do not collect personal data, our hosting provider may automatically log certain technical details when you visit our site. These server log files can include information such as:

  • IP address
  • Date and time of access
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL (the previous page you visited)

These logs are typically maintained by the hosting provider for diagnostic, security, and statistical purposes. However, we do not use this data to identify or track individuals. We only access these logs to ensure site functionality, detect potential security threats, and maintain the website.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website does not embed any third-party services (e.g., social media plugins, video embeds, or analytics) that automatically collect personal information. Should we integrate any new third-party service in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and clearly disclose how these services might collect and process your data.

As part of our commitment to privacy, we regularly review potential third-party integrations to ensure they align with our privacy standards and values.

5. Data Security

While we do not collect personal information, we take reasonable precautions to protect our website’s infrastructure and any server logs maintained by our hosting provider. These measures aim to mitigate the risk of unauthorized access, data breaches, or other forms of security threats.

Our security practices may include the use of encryption protocols, secure network configurations, regular updates and patches, and internal access controls to ensure only authorized personnel can manage our website content.

6. International Data Transfers

Our website is hosted in Switzerland, and any server logs or operational data are processed in accordance with Swiss data protection laws. We do not intentionally transfer any user data outside of Switzerland. In the event that any form of data transfer becomes necessary in the future, we will ensure that the data is adequately protected under the relevant legal frameworks and data protection regulations.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to update, request deletion for, or opt out of. If, however, you believe that our practices might involve your personal information in some way, you may contact us to address any questions or concerns.

We respect the privacy rights of all our visitors and remain committed to providing transparency regarding our data handling processes.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed toward children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16. Since we do not gather any personal data at all, this measure reinforces our dedication to protecting the privacy of vulnerable users.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

The updated version will be posted on our website, and the “Last updated” date at the top will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
